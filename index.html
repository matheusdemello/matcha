Matcha Brewing Guide

Traditional Usucha (Thin Tea): Sift 1.5-2g (approx. 1/2-3/4 tsp) matcha into bowl. Add 70-80ml (2.5-3oz) water at 175-185Â°F (80-85Â°C). Whisk in "W" or "M" motion until frothy.

Traditional Koicha (Thick Tea): Sift 3-4g (approx. 1-1.5 tsp) matcha into bowl. Add 30-40ml (1-1.5oz) water at 175-185Â°F (80-85Â°C). Knead with chasen (bamboo whisk) until smooth and glossy, no froth.

Modern Preparation: For lattes, smoothies, or cold brew, adjust quantities based on desired strength and other ingredients.

Storage Tip: Always store matcha in airtight containers away from light, heat, moisture, and strong odors. Refrigeration recommended after opening.
